An interactive online design thinking course.

Curated keeping you in mind

We created this program to give your team hands-on experience using the tools and techniques of the design thinking approach to benefit your organization.

Our design thinking training program (which we offer online only) is geared towards direct business impact. You will learn the design thinking methodology using examples tailored to your organization.

This course is most relevant for product managers, sales, marketing, leadership, engineers, business developers, and trainees. You can select from the different modules. Talk to our team to build a program that matches your needs.

Module 1: What is Design Thinking?

Idea to Inspire goes through the five phases of design thinking, showing how easily you can create prototypes. We begin with the basic definition of design thinking and explain all the different phases of design thinking. Then we explore inspiring Design Thinking cases and examples.


Module 2: How to use Design Thinking?

Learn by doing! This training module focuses on the problem phase of design thinking. Participants learn what it's like to actively empathise with customers and generate useful insights. The team will learn to master the skills of ideation on the second training day.

Module 3: Experimentation & Prototyping

In this section of our design thinking workshop, we teach you how to construct various experiments to test your solution ideas. We start by emphasising the assumptions that can make or break your idea. Following that, we create a solution experiment with digital experimentation tools and devise a validation strategy.

Module 4: Facilitator workshop

In this section of our workshop, we'll show you how to develop design thinking capabilities within your own company. It is not necessary to have prior knowledge of design thinking. You will learn by doing in this train-the-trainer module! Everything on the agenda for the workshop will be presented/taught by participants, with feedback from the facilitator and the group. Participants will gain facilitation and design thinking skills, as well as the ability to answer key questions.
